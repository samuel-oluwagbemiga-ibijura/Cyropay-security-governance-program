# Executive Risk Dashboard — CyroPay Ltd

**Project type:** GRC Portfolio · Risk Reporting & Executive Communication  
**Organisation:** CyroPay Ltd (fictional UK payment fintech)  
**Prepared by:** Samuel O. Ibijura · MSc Cybersecurity  
**Date:** May 2026

---

## Overview

An executive-level information security risk dashboard consolidating findings from all four preceding projects into a single management view. The dashboard is designed for presentation to a board or senior leadership team — translating technical GRC findings into business language with clear risk ratings, compliance metrics, and recommended actions.

---

## Dashboard Contents

### Executive Dashboard (Excel)
- **KPI Scorecard** — 9 key metrics: total controls assessed, compliance rate, risk counts by severity, policies published
- **Compliance by Category** — A.5 through A.8 breakdown with compliant/non-compliant/partial counts and percentages
- **Clustered Bar Chart** — Visual comparison of compliance status across all four Annex A categories
- **Risk Distribution Pie Chart** — Critical, High, Medium risk breakdown
- **Top 10 Risk Register Summary** — All risks with scores, ratings, owners, and target dates
- **Vendor Risk Summary** — AWS, Stripe, Salesforce scores and risk ratings
- **Policy Register Tracker** — All 10 policies showing Active status and review dates

### CISO Briefing (Word)
A board-ready one-page briefing covering:
- KPI scorecard
- Compliance summary table with colour-coded findings
- Key findings in Critical/High/Medium/Positive format
- Priority risk remediation plan with target dates
- CISO recommendation to the Board
