# Information Security Policy Suite — CyroPay Ltd

**Project type:** GRC Portfolio · Governance Documentation  
**Organisation:** CyroPay Ltd (fictional UK payment fintech)  
**Prepared by:** Samuel O. Ibijura · MSc Cybersecurity  
**Date:** May 2026

---

## Overview

A complete suite of 10 mandatory information security policies developed for CyroPay Ltd, customised for the UK financial services regulatory context including FCA SYSC requirements, UK GDPR, and PCI DSS obligations.

Each policy is mapped to its relevant ISO 27001:2022 Annex A controls and maintained in a version-controlled policy register.

## Policy Structure

Each policy follows a consistent structure:
1. Purpose
2. Scope
3. Definitions
4. Roles and Responsibilities
5. Policy Requirements
6. Compliance and Monitoring
7. Exceptions
8. Related Documents
9. ISO 27001 Annex A Mapping
10. Document Control

All policies are version 1.0, effective May 2026, review date May 2027.
